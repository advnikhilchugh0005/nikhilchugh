Introduction

Nikhil Chugh (born 23 August 1999) is an Indian Advocate, legal educator, and public speaker based in Panipat, Haryana. He is a Member of the Supreme Court Bar Association and practices primarily in criminal law, bail matters, constitutional litigation and cheque bounce cases under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act.

Apart from litigation practice, he is also known for promoting legal awareness through digital platforms where he explains legal rights, criminal procedure and constitutional protections to the general public.

Early Life and Background

Nikhil Chugh was born on 23 August 1999 in Panipat, Haryana, India. He completed most of his early schooling from GD Goenka School, Panipat. Later, he pursued his senior secondary education in the medical stream from Delhi.

Education

  • BA.LLB (Hons.) – Chandigarh University (2018–2023)
  • LL.M in Constitutional Law – Kurukshetra University (2023–2025)
  • MBA in Finance – Kurukshetra University (2024–2026)
  • Qualified UGC NET

Legal Career

Nikhil Chugh enrolled with the Bar Council of Punjab and Haryana in 2023 and began practicing law primarily before the District Courts of Panipat. His practice includes representation in criminal defence matters, bail proceedings, constitutional writ petitions and commercial litigation.

He is also associated with matters connected to the Punjab & Haryana High Court and legal proceedings involving the Supreme Court of India.

Areas of Practice

  • Criminal Law
  • Bail Matters
  • Section 138 Negotiable Instruments Act (Cheque Bounce Cases)
  • Constitutional Law
  • Trial Court Litigation
  • High Court Litigation

Professional Memberships

  • Member – Supreme Court Bar Association
  • Enrolled Advocate – Bar Council of Punjab & Haryana
  • Practicing Advocate – District Court Panipat

Professional Roles

Nikhil Chugh also serves as a Legal Research Officer with the All India Council of Human Rights, Liberties & Social Justice (AICHLS). In addition to his institutional role, he practices independently as an advocate in litigation matters.

Legal Awareness and Media Presence

Alongside courtroom practice, Nikhil Chugh actively promotes legal awareness through social media platforms where he explains citizens’ rights, criminal law procedures and constitutional protections. His educational legal reels and digital content have reached a wide audience online.

He has published numerous legal awareness articles and guides on his official website addressing common legal issues such as FIR procedure, bail law, rights of the accused and cheque bounce cases.

Public Speaking

Nikhil Chugh is also known as a young legal orator and public speaker who participates in discussions relating to constitutional law, legal education and public legal awareness.

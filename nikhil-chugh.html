Biography of Nikhil Chugh

Nikhil Chugh (born 23 August 1999) is an Indian advocate, legal educator and author based in Panipat, Haryana. He is a member of the Supreme Court Bar Association and practices primarily in criminal law, bail matters, constitutional litigation and cheque bounce cases under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act.

Early Life

Nikhil Chugh was born in Panipat, Haryana. He completed his schooling primarily from GD Goenka School, Panipat and later pursued senior secondary education in Delhi.

He is also known as Nikhil Chugh, Advocate in legal and public speaking circles.

Published Works

Nikhil Chugh is the author of the legal awareness book Legal Rights Every Citizen Must Know , which explains FIR procedure, bail law, police powers and constitutional safeguards under India's new criminal laws including the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) and Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita (BNSS).

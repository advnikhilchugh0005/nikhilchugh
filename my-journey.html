From NEET Qualifier to Advocate: Why I Chose Justice Over Medicine

“A doctor heals individuals, but the law can transform society. That belief changed my life.”

My name is Nikhil Chugh, an Advocate, Author, Legal Educator, and Ph.D. Scholar from Panipat, Haryana. My journey from medical science to law has been driven by a simple mission—making justice and legal awareness accessible to every citizen.

🏡 Early Life

Born and raised in Panipat, Haryana, I grew up with values of education, discipline, integrity, and service to society. These principles continue to guide my professional journey.

🩺 My Medical Background

During Classes XI and XII, I pursued the medical stream and successfully qualified the NEET examination. At that stage, medicine appeared to be my future.

However, my growing interest in constitutional values, governance, public policy, and citizens' rights gradually led me toward the field of law.

⚖️ Choosing Law Over Medicine

Leaving the medical path despite qualifying NEET was one of the most difficult decisions of my life.

I deeply respect the medical profession, but I realized that my passion lay in protecting rights, promoting justice, and strengthening legal awareness.

Looking back, I have never regretted that decision.

🎓 Academic Journey

  • BA.LLB (Hons.)
  • LL.M. in Constitutional Law
  • MBA in Finance
  • UGC NET Qualified
  • Pursuing Ph.D. in Law

📚 Why Legal Awareness Became My Mission

my-journey.html

During my interactions with citizens, I realized that many people are unaware of their most basic legal rights.

This inspired me to dedicate a significant part of my work to legal literacy and public legal education.

👨‍⚖️ Professional Work

Today, I practice law, engage in legal research, contribute to public legal education, and pursue advanced legal scholarship through my Ph.D.

My areas of interest include Constitutional Law, Criminal Law, Human Rights, and Legal Awareness.

📖 Author & Legal Educator

Legal Rights Every Citizen Must Know

One of the most meaningful milestones of my journey has been authoring a book that simplifies FIR procedures, bail, police powers, and citizens' rights under India's new criminal laws.

🏆 Recognition & Outreach

Copies of my book have reached the Prime Minister's Office, Rashtrapati Bhavan, Members of Parliament, MLAs, District Commissioners, Superintendents of Police, and various educational institutions.

However, the true success of the work lies in helping citizens understand their rights.

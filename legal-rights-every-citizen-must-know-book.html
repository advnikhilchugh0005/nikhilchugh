By Nikhil Chugh, Advocate | Supreme Court Bar Association Member | Panipat District Court

About the Book

Legal Rights Every Citizen Must Know is written by Advocate Nikhil Chugh, a practicing criminal and constitutional lawyer based in Panipat, Haryana.

Legal Rights Every Citizen Must Know is a practical legal awareness guide explaining important aspects of criminal procedure in India including FIR registration, arrest procedures, bail law and police powers.

The book also explains constitutional safeguards available to citizens under Articles 20, 21 and 22 of the Constitution of India and highlights the changes introduced through India’s new criminal laws such as the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS), Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita (BNSS) and Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam (BSA).

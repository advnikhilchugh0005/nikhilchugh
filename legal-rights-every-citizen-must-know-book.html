About the Book

"Legal Rights Every Citizen Must Know" is written by Advocate Nikhil Chugh, a practicing criminal and constitutional lawyer based in Panipat, Haryana.

The book is a practical legal awareness guide that explains important aspects of criminal procedure in India, including FIR registration, arrest procedures, bail law and the legal powers of the police under Indian law.

The book also explains constitutional safeguards available to citizens under Articles 20, 21 and 22 of the Constitution of India and highlights the major reforms introduced through India's new criminal laws such as the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS), Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita (BNSS) and the Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam (BSA).

Through simple explanations and practical insights, "Legal Rights Every Citizen Must Know" aims to make legal rights and criminal procedure easier to understand for students, citizens and individuals seeking legal awareness.

Nikhil Chugh

Nikhil Chugh is an Indian advocate, legal educator and author based in Panipat, Haryana, and a member of the Supreme Court Bar Association. He is known for simplifying citizens’ legal rights including FIR, bail and police procedure under India’s new criminal laws such as the BNSS 2023.

Following the release of his book Legal Rights Every Citizen Must Know, several digital news platforms featured his work promoting legal literacy among citizens.

This media coverage establishes Nikhil Chugh as a recognised legal educator and public legal awareness advocate in India.

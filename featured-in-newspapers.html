Advocate Nikhil Chugh Featured in Leading Newspapers

Legal Awareness Book on FIR, Bail and Police Procedure under BNSS 2023

Nikhil Chugh is an Indian advocate, legal educator and author based in Panipat, Haryana, and a member of the Supreme Court Bar Association.

He has recently been featured in prominent Hindi newspapers for his efforts in spreading legal awareness among the general public through his work on FIR, bail, and police procedures under India's new criminal laws.

📘 About the Book

Nikhil Chugh is the author of “Legal Rights Every Citizen Must Know: FIR, Bail and Police Procedure Under India's New Criminal Laws (BNSS 2023)”.

The book simplifies complex legal procedures and explains essential rights that every citizen should be aware of in a practical and easy-to-understand manner.

About Nikhil Chugh – Advocate in Panipat, Haryana

Nikhil Chugh is a practicing Advocate in Panipat, Haryana and a Member of the Supreme Court Bar Association. He holds a BA.LLB (Hons.), LL.M in Constitutional Law, and MBA in Finance. His practice focuses on Constitutional Law, Criminal Law, Bail Matters, and Section 138 Negotiable Instruments Act cases. He regularly represents clients before Trial Courts in Panipat, the Punjab & Haryana High Court, and in matters connected to the Supreme Court of India. Known for his strategic litigation approach and constitutional precision, he provides professional legal advisory and courtroom representation across civil and criminal disputes. As a criminal lawyer in Panipat and a constitutional law practitioner, he is committed to delivering ethical, result-oriented, and client-focused legal services.

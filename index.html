About Nikhil Chugh – Advocate in Panipat, Haryana

Nikhil Chugh is a practicing Criminal & Civil Lawyer in Panipat and a Member of the Supreme Court Bar Association. He regularly represents clients in criminal & Civil litigation, bail matters, constitutional law issues, and cheque bounce cases under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act.

Advocate Nikhil Chugh practices primarily before the District Courts in Panipat and is also involved in matters connected with the Punjab & Haryana High Court and the Supreme Court of India. He focuses on providing strategic legal representation in criminal & Civil defence, bail applications, and complex constitutional issues.

Apart from courtroom practice, he actively promotes legal awareness through digital platforms and publishes legal articles explaining the rights of citizens under Indian law.
